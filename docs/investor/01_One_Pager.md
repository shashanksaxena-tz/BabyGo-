# TinySteps AI — Investor One Pager
**Studio Venture Pitch | Feb 2026**

---

## What We've Built

TinySteps AI is a **child development intelligence platform** for parents of children aged 0–6. It combines AI-powered milestone tracking, WHO-standard growth analytics, personalised bedtime stories, recipe suggestions, and development insights — all driven by each child's unique profile.

The product is **live** across Web (React) and Mobile (Flutter), with a Node.js/MongoDB backend, Gemini AI integration, and a Docker-based deployment stack already running.

---

## The Gap We're Filling

Most baby apps are **content libraries** — they broadcast generic advice. TinySteps AI is the first Indian platform that **knows your child** and personalises every experience around them.

The pediatrician channel is a strong first-mover. But it is *one* distribution lever in a market with many.

---

## The Bigger Opportunity

### 1. Brand Marketplace (B2B2C)
Indian baby product brands — FirstCry, MamaEarth, Mee Mee, R for Rabbit and 50+ others — sell products but have **zero personalisation layer**. TinySteps AI can be that layer:
- Recommend products **by the child's exact age, weight, and milestone stage**
- Brands pay for **contextual placement** (not blind ads)
- Revenue: brand partnership fees and commissions

### 2. Schools & Daycares — Distribution & Promotion
India has 200,000+ preschools and daycares (EuroKids, Kidzee, Bachpan, KLAY, independents). These are high-trust environments where parents are already engaged and thinking about their child's development.
- Set up **kiosks, booths, and demo stations** at school events and parent-teacher days
- Get teachers to **recommend and endorse** TinySteps AI to parents
- Run **referral programs** through school communication channels (WhatsApp groups, newsletters, notice boards)
- Host **parent workshops** demonstrating the app's developmental tracking capabilities
- Use school premises for **on-premise branding** — welcome kits, QR codes on notice boards, branded milestone cards

### 3. TinySteps Box — Activity Subscription (D2C)
Every 3 months, a curated **activity kit** arrives at the doorstep — personalised to the child's current milestones, upcoming developmental stage, and interests.
- Product curation powered by TinySteps AI data
- Fulfillment partnerships with existing baby product logistics players (FirstCry, Amazon, D2C brands)
- Revenue: subscription fees and brand co-creation partnerships

---

## Why This Compounds

Each vertical **feeds the others**:

```
Child profile data → better brand recommendations → brand pays →
schools promote app to parents → more parents → richer data →
better activity boxes → box ships brand products → brand pays more
```

The data flywheel makes TinySteps AI **harder to replicate** over time.
