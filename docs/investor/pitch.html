The Platform

What We've Built

TinySteps AI is a child development intelligence platform for parents of children aged 0–6. It combines AI-powered milestone tracking, WHO-standard growth analytics, personalised stories, recipes, and development insights — all driven by each child’s unique profile.

The Opportunity

The Gap We're Filling

Most baby apps are content libraries — they broadcast generic advice to all parents. TinySteps AI is the first Indian platform that knows your child and personalises every experience around their unique development journey.

❌ Today's Baby Apps

  • Generic content for "parents of 6-month-olds"
  • No awareness of individual child's milestones
  • Cookie-cutter product recommendations
  • No connection between home and school
  • One-size-fits-all activity suggestions

✓ TinySteps AI

  • Knows each child's exact developmental stage
  • Personalised stories, recipes, insights per child
  • Milestone-aware product recommendations
  • Trusted by pediatricians as a referral tool
  • AI that gets smarter as the child grows

The Market Opportunity

India's baby care ecosystem — spanning products, education, nutrition, and services — is one of the fastest-growing consumer segments. The pediatrician channel is a strong first-mover, but it is one distribution lever in a market with many. TinySteps AI is positioned to become the intelligence layer that connects parents, brands, schools, and healthcare providers around every child's development.

Growth Strategy

Expansion Pillars

Five interconnected pillars that compound together. Each vertical feeds the others, creating a data flywheel that strengthens over time.

Pillar 01

Pediatrician & Healthcare Network

Active Focus

Partner with pediatricians as trusted referral sources. When a doctor recommends TinySteps AI at a checkup, it carries the highest trust signal for new parents — creating quality installs, not just downloads.

  • Pediatrician Dashboard — Doctors can view progress of their referred patients
  • Secure Reporting — Share child development data with the doctor securely
  • Vaccine & Growth Tracking — Integrated into the pediatric workflow
  • Hospital Networks — Scale through child wellness programs at hospital chains
Pillar 02

Brand Marketplace

Highest Near-Term Revenue

India's baby product brands spend heavily on generic ads. They have no way to know: Is this parent buying for a 6-month-old or a 2-year-old? Is the child walking yet? Does she need fine motor skill toys or gross motor? TinySteps AI knows all of this.

What We Offer Brands

  • Contextual Product Placement — Recommend the exact right product at the exact right developmental moment
  • Milestone-Triggered Campaigns — When thousands of children reach "first steps" milestone, the walking shoe brand can send a timely promotion
  • Co-Branded Content — Brands fund creation of activity guides and stories featuring their products
  • Anonymised Insights — Aggregate milestone data tells brands what real parents at each age stage experience
Toys & Learning Baby Nutrition Clothing Baby Gear Skincare Books
Pillar 03

Schools & Daycares — Distribution & Promotion

Distribution Channel

India has over 200,000 preschools and daycares, each with dozens to hundreds of children enrolled. These are the highest concentration of our target audience in a single location. Rather than building software for schools, we leverage them as physical distribution and promotion channels.

Distribution Strategy

  • Kiosks & Demo Stations — Physical TinySteps AI presence at school events, orientations, and open days. Parents experience the app firsthand while they’re already thinking about their child’s development.
  • Teacher Endorsements — Partner with schools so teachers recommend TinySteps AI to parents. A teacher’s recommendation carries immense trust — comparable to a pediatrician’s.
  • School Referral Programs — Schools participate in referral programs, driving app adoption through their parent communication channels — WhatsApp groups, newsletters, parent-teacher meetings.
  • Parent Workshops — Host child development awareness sessions at schools, demonstrating the app’s capabilities to an already-engaged audience.
  • On-Premise Branding — Notice board placements, flyers in welcome kits, QR codes in school communications — every touchpoint drives installs.
Schools are where the parents are. A single school partnership gives us access to hundreds of families who are actively invested in their child’s development — the exact audience that converts best.
Pillar 04

TinySteps Box — Personalised Activity Subscription

On Roadmap

A curated box of activities arrives quarterly, personalised to the child’s exact developmental stage, upcoming milestones, logged interests, and seasonal relevance. The Box is the physical manifestation of the digital intelligence.

What’s In the Box

  • Activity Kits — Puzzles, sensory toys, art materials matched to current milestones
  • Personalised Story Booklet — Custom story featuring the child's name, printed
  • Recipe Card — Age-appropriate recipe to cook together
  • Milestone Guide — "Your child is about to learn X — here’s how to help"
  • Brand Products — Co-branded product inclusions (brands pay placement + product featured)

Key Differentiator: Existing boxes (Flintobox, Intellikit) send the same kit to all children of an age group. TinySteps Box uses real-time developmental data to make every box truly personal.

Pillar 05

Parent Community & Premium

Partially Built

Community creates retention that no algorithm can replicate. Parents with children of the same age are each other’s most trusted advisors.

  • Milestone Celebrations — Share achievements with a supportive parent community
  • Expert Consultations — Book video calls with pediatricians, child psychologists, nutritionists, physiotherapists
  • Premium Subscription — Unlimited AI features, full reports, priority support
  • Content Partnerships — Parenting courses, expert-created guides, age-specific content

The Flywheel

More parents → Richer profiles → Better personalisation → Brands pay more → Revenue funds better AI → Schools promote → Box gets smarter → More parents join

Each pillar reinforces the others. The moat is the data + trust that accumulates over time.

Revenue Model

Five Revenue Streams

Multiple monetisation paths that grow with the platform. The model shifts from B2C subscription in early phase to a multi-sided marketplace at scale.

Stream 01

B2C Premium Subscription

Freemium model — basic milestone tracking is free, premium unlocks unlimited AI features, full development reports, and priority support. Family tier supports multiple children.

  • Freemium to paid conversion
  • Individual and family tiers
  • Pediatrician referrals lower CAC
Stream 02

Brand Marketplace

Brands pay for precision access to parents at the exact developmental moment their product is needed. Multiple engagement models from simple presence to deep integration.

  • Brand presence and listing
  • Performance-based commissions
  • Exclusive milestone sponsorships
  • Anonymised trend reports
Stream 03

School Distribution Partnerships

Schools serve as distribution channels, driving high-quality installs through referral programs, kiosk placements, and teacher endorsements. Partnerships structured around mutual benefit.

  • Referral-based partnerships
  • Kiosk and event presence fees
  • Co-branded parent engagement
Stream 04

TinySteps Box

Quarterly subscription delivering personalised activity kits. Dual revenue from parent subscriptions and brand co-creation fees for product inclusion.

  • Quarterly and annual subscriptions
  • Gift box options
  • Premium personalised tier
  • Brand co-creation placement
Stream 05

Expert Consultations

Parents book video consultations with pediatricians, child psychologists, nutritionists, and physiotherapists. Platform earns a commission on each booking.

  • Booking commission model
  • Expert network across specialties
  • Integrated with child's profile data

Why This Model Compounds

The first brand deal closes → creates a case study → makes the next 10 brand deals easier. Each new parent on the platform increases the value of brand placements. Each school partnership drives concentrated, high-quality installs. The subscription box creates physical touchpoints that increase digital retention. Every stream amplifies the others.
